Report a Sighting

If you’ve had an encounter, we want the details while they’re still fresh. Reports can be submitted publicly or kept private upon request.

  • Exact location (nearest town, county, and coordinates if possible)
  • Date, time, weather, and visibility conditions
  • Distance, direction of travel, and how long you observed it
  • Sounds, footprints, odors, or other physical evidence
  • Photos, audio, video, or sketches (if available)
Submit a Report → How we review reports

Please don’t trespass or put yourself at risk to gather evidence. If you believe someone is in immediate danger, contact local authorities.

Before you submit

Write down what you remember

1 Mark the location and your line of sight.
2 Note any sounds (whoops, knocks, movement).
3 If tracks exist, photograph with a size reference.
We may follow up for clarification if you include contact info.
